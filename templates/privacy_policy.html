Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Nomad MemorAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your email address when you register to creates a secure account for syncing your project data.
  • Project Data (User Content): We store the project memories, notes, and rules that you explicitly choose to save via the extension.
  • Website Content: The extension accesses the text content of supported AI chat interfaces (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini) ONLY when you trigger the "Save Memory" or "Inject Context" actions. We do not passively track your browsing history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of injecting your project context into AI chats.
  • Authenticate your identity and sync your data across devices.
  • Improve the accuracy of memory retrieval algorithms.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway.app. We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure token-based authentication, to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. Your project data is only shared with the AI platforms (e.g., OpenAI, Google) when you explicitly inject it into a chat prompt.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time directly through the extension interface or by contacting us.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please create an issue on our GitHub repository.
